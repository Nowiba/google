Our Story

More Than Just a Haircut

Blade & Bristle was founded on the belief that a trip to the barber should be an experience, not just an errand. We blend time-honored techniques with contemporary style, creating a space where tradition meets modern sophistication.

Our passion lies in the details – the perfect fade, the sharpest line-up, the most relaxing hot towel shave. We're dedicated to providing exceptional service and building lasting relationships with our clients.
